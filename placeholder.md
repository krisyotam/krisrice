This is the self-hosted personal git of kris yotam (krisyotam.com). All of my personal projects are slowly being moved over to this from my [github](https://github.com/krisyotam). If there is something you'd like me to host you think would interest me contact me at krisyotam@protonmail.com

I am a Writer, Programmer, and Critic who caters to the [Avant-Garde](https://krisyotam.com). I [research](https://krisyotam.com/papers) niche topics, write [literary essays](https://krisyotam.com/essays) reflecting on the many facets of 
life, [blog](https://krisyotam.com/blog), keep long form [notes](https://krisyotam.com/notes), and my 
[working notes](https://notes.krisyotam.com) public. I am fairly reclusive online, you won't see me freqeunt public squares. I am active on [IRC](https://tools.suckless.org/sic/), discord, reddit, and some forums like 
[LessWrong](https://www.lesswrong.com/users/krisyotam), [Effective Altruism Forum](https://forum.effectivealtruism.org/users/krisyotam), and 
the [AI Alignment Forum](https://www.alignmentforum.org/users/krisyotam). I also write [verse](https://krisyotam.com/verse), and practice via the [progymnasmata](https://krisyotam.com/progymnasmata).
